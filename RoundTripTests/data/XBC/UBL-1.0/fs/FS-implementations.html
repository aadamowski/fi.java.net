1.  Known implementations of UBL formatting specifications

This document records pointers to implementations of UBL formatting specifications that were made known to the UBL Forms Processing Subcommittee at the time of publishing (noted above).

None of these implementations may be considered as reference implementations of UBL formatting specifications or as normative components of the UBL delivery; they are merely examples from what will probably be many available UBL stylesheet libraries.

2.  Reported implementations or intentions of implementing 1.0 specifications

The following are announced packages that now support or have been announced to support the 1.0 release of the UBL package.

  • XSLT and XSL-FO stylesheets in clear text from Crane Softwrights Ltd.:

    • http://www.CraneSoftwrights.com/links/res-ublo.htm

  • Java JAR standalone implementation of Crane's XSLT and XSL-FO stylesheets from Ambrosoft, Inc.:

    • http://www.ambrosoft.com

  • XForms implementations from Micah Dubinko:

    • http://dubinko.info/writing/xforms/ubl/

  • XSLT and ZPT to HTML/PDF implementations from Accountis plc:

    • http://www.accountis.org/

3.  Web site record

As part of the FPSC mandate, a record of known implementations of UBL Formatting Specifications will be made public and maintained on the OASIS web site.
